# Presentation Title: Accelerating PM Surya Ghar Adoption to 1 Crore Homes

**Target Audience:** Joint Secretary, MNRE & PM Surya Ghar Implementation Task Force
**Presenter:** Naman Garg, Founder of UrjaGram
**Core Value Proposition:** A unified Digital Aggregator platform reducing the installation-to-subsidy lifecycle from 60+ days to under 15 days using automated financing, RPA integration, and strict DCR compliance enforcement.

---

## Slide 1: The Bottleneck in the 1 Crore Goal
*The PM Surya Ghar scheme has massive intent, but ground-level execution is slowed down by friction.*
- **Consumer Friction:** Capital constraints. Finding ₹2-3 Lakhs upfront is difficult for semi-urban and rural households.
- **Vendor Opacity:** Consumers struggle to find reliable, empanelled vendors.
- **Processing Delays:** Manual data entry across multiple DISCOMs and the National Portal leads to a high rejection rate for subsidies (e.g., Aadhaar/Bank name mismatches).

---

## Slide 2: Introducing UrjaGram
*A "Zero-Defect" Aggregation Platform built for the PM Surya Ghar ecosystem.*
- **Pre-Emptive Quality Check:** AI-driven document scanning catches Aadhaar-Bank mismatches *before* they are submitted to the government portal.
- **Instant Digital Lending (LaaS):** We have integrated Green NBFC APIs directly into the vendor selection process. Consumers get 1-click loan approvals, so vendors get paid faster and work starts immediately.
- **100% DCR Compliance:** Our system mathematically enforces that only Domestic Content Requirement (DCR) certified modules are quoted by vendors, guaranteeing 0% subsidy rejection due to non-compliant hardware.

---

## Slide 3: The Architecture of Speed
*How UrjaGram cuts the timeline by 70%.*
1. **AI Demand Sizing:** Consumers upload a rooftop photo. Our AI computes the optimal system size and estimates the exact MNRE subsidy in 10 seconds.
2. **Smart Vendor Matchmaking:** The platform only connects users with actively empanelled vendors who have verified DCR inventory ready to deploy.
3. **Parallel Financing:** While DISCOM feasibility is pending, UrjaGram secures the consumer's loan from a partner NBFC. The vendor receives the first tranche instantly upon DISCOM approval.

---

## Slide 4: Zero-Defect GOI Sync (The Tech)
*We do not replace the National Portal; we supercharge it.*
- UrjaGram currently utilizes secure Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to push verified, error-free application data directly into `pmsuryaghar.gov.in` on behalf of empanelled vendors.
- This ensures that when MNRE and DISCOM officials review applications coming from UrjaGram vendors, the approval rate is near 100%.

---

## Slide 5: The "Ask" from the Ministry
*To scale this impact nationwide, we seek a strategic partnership.*
1. **Official Digital Aggregator Recognition:** Recognition under the scheme to build trust with consumers when collecting KYC data for financing.
2. **Direct API Access (G2B):** Transitioning from RPA to direct, secure REST API integration with the National Portal for real-time application submission and subsidy status tracking.
3. **Pilot Program:** Authorization to run a closed-loop pilot in a high-priority state to prove a 50% reduction in installation time.
